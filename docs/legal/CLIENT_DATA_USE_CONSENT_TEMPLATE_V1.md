# Client Data Use Consent Template V1

> Template only. Must be reviewed and adapted by qualified local legal/professional counsel before use.

## Purpose

This template explains how the clinic may use client and pet information when
using Pet-Med-AI as a clinical assistance and operations tool.

## Suggested client-facing wording

I understand that the clinic may record and process information about me, my pet,
and my pet's clinical care for the following purposes:

```txt
creating and maintaining pet medical records
supporting veterinary consultation and clinical documentation
summarizing case history for veterinarian review
using AI-assisted tools to organize clinical information
generating draft clinical documents for veterinarian review
managing preventive care reminders
managing front-desk manual contact workflows
maintaining audit, release, backup and operational safety records
```

## Data that may be used

```txt
client / pet owner name
client / pet owner contact information
pet name

history

clinician review/audit records
```

## AI-assisted workflow notice

I understand that Pet-Med-AI may help the clinic organize information and draft
suggestions, but:

```txt
AI output is not a final diagnosis.
AI output is not a prescription.
AI output does not replace a veterinarian's clinical judgment.
The attending veterinarian or clinic clinician is responsible for final clinical decisions.
Emergency or worsening conditions require direct veterinary care.
```

## Preventive reminder notice

I understand that preventive care reminders are convenience reminders. They are
not diagnosis, treatment, prescription or emergency triage.

Commercial V1 external client contact remains manual. The system must not
automatically send SMS, WeChat or email reminders in Commercial V1.

## Data sharing

The clinic should not share client data with third parties unless:

```txt
the client has been informed where required
there is a documented clinical/operational purpose
legal/professional review allows it
appropriate security controls exist
```

Commercial V1 does not require external SMS, WeChat, email, EMR write, DICOM,
device gateway or payment provider credentials.

## Opt-out / withdrawal

I may request to opt out of reminder/contact workflows where applicable.

Opt-out may affect convenience reminder services, but the clinic should still
provide necessary clinical care according to clinic policy and applicable rules.
