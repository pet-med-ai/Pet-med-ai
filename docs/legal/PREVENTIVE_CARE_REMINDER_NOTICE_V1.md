# Preventive Care Reminder Notice V1

> Template only. Must be reviewed and adapted by qualified local legal/professional counsel before use.

## Purpose

This notice explains how preventive care reminders work in Pet-Med-AI Commercial V1.

## Commercial V1 reminder boundary

Pet-Med-AI may help the clinic create and manage preventive care reminders for:

```txt
vaccination-related follow-up
deworming-related follow-up
general preventive care review
front-desk manual contact queue
```

Commercial V1 reminders are:

```txt
in-app / clinic workflow reminders
reviewed by clinic staff
manual contact only
not automatic external sending
```

## Non-diagnosis / non-prescription statement

Preventive care reminders are not:

```txt
a diagnosis
a prescription
a treatment order
an emergency alert system
a guarantee that a procedure is clinically appropriate for every patient
```

The veterinarian or clinic clinician must decide whether a preventive care action
is appropriate for the specific pet.

## No automated external sending in Commercial V1

Commercial V1 must not automatically send:

```txt
SMS
WeChat
email
external provider messages
```

Front-desk staff may manually contact clients according to clinic policy and
client preferences/opt-out records.

## Opt-out

Clients may request opt-out from reminder/contact workflows where applicable.

Staff must record opt-out and stop non-essential reminder/contact workflow for
that client according to the clinic's policy.

## Emergency warning

Preventive care reminders must not be used for emergencies. If a pet is unwell
or worsening, the client should contact the clinic or seek direct veterinary care
according to clinic instructions.

## Staff rules

```txt
Check client opt-out before contact.
Do not contact clients through automated external sending in Commercial V1.
Do not describe reminders as diagnosis or prescription.
Do not override clinician judgment.
Record manual contact status accurately.
```
