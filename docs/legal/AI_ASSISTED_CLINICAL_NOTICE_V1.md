# AI Assisted Clinical Notice V1

> Template only. Must be reviewed and adapted by qualified local legal/professional counsel before use.

## Purpose

This notice explains the role and limits of AI assistance in Pet-Med-AI.

## Core statement

Pet-Med-AI is an AI-assisted clinical workflow tool used by veterinary
professionals. It may help collect structured history, summarize case information,
suggest possible risk areas, draft differential diagnosis ideas, suggest checks,
and prepare clinical documentation drafts.

Pet-Med-AI does not replace the veterinarian.

## Veterinarian final responsibility statement

```txt
The attending veterinarian / clinic clinician remains responsible for final
clinical judgment, diagnosis, treatment decisions, medication decisions,
procedures, client communication and follow-up planning.
```

## AI limitations

AI-generated content may be:

```txt
incomplete
overconfident
outdated
not specific enough for the individual patient
incorrect if the input history is incomplete or wrong
unsafe if copied directly without clinical review
```

## Required clinical review

Before AI output is saved into a case or used in clinical communication:

```txt
a clinician must review it
incorrect content must be corrected or rejected
important uncertainty must be documented
client-facing advice must be checked by a veterinarian/clinician
high-risk cases must not rely on AI output alone
```

## Not for emergency replacement

Pet-Med-AI should not be used as a replacement for direct emergency veterinary
care. Worsening, severe or urgent clinical signs require direct veterinarian
assessment according to clinic policy.

## Not an autonomous prescribing system

Commercial V1 must not be represented as:

```txt
an autonomous prescription system
a medication order writer
a final diagnosis engine
a replacement for physical exam
a replacement for laboratory or imaging interpretation by qualified staff
```

## Staff acknowledgement

Clinic staff using Pet-Med-AI should acknowledge:

```txt
I understand that AI output is assistive only.
I will review AI output before relying on it.
I will not send AI-generated medical advice to clients without appropriate clinical review.
I will not represent Pet-Med-AI as replacing a veterinarian.
I will report unsafe or incorrect AI output through the clinic's review process.
```
